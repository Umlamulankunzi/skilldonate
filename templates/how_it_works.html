How it works

This guide explains how our website functions! We aim to give you an overview of how the platform works and how it can work for you to achieve great things.

Volunteers

Volunteers who wish to engage Charity Organisations by donating their skills and time, can use this Skill donate platform, to register a simple account and start offer their skills for donation or they can also offer to donate to skill requirements posted by Charity Organisations on the platform, by showing their interest, or contacting the Organisation.

As a Volunteer you can:

  • Create an account: Sign up as a volunteer on skilldonate by providing your personal information and creating a secure login. Once registered as a volunteer, you'll have full access to the skilldonate platform and its features.
  • Browse Skill Requirements: As a volunteer you can browse through the skill requirements posted by non-profit organizations. These requirements represent the specific skills they are seeking assistance with. If you find a match for a skill you possess, you can show your interest by indicating that you can help. This will Let non-profits know that you're available to contribute your expertise.
  • Post Skill Donations: Alternatively, Volunteers can post the specific skills they would like to donate on the platform and wait for Charity Organisations to show interest in the donated skills.

Charity Organisations

Charity Organisations who are seeking donations in the form of skills from skilled volunteers can register on the platform and connect with volunteers wishing to donate their skills. Charity Organisations, you can also browse donated skills posted by volunteers organizations. These skills are skills which volunteers are willing to donate to Charity Organisations.

As a Charity Organisation you can:

  • Create an account: Charity organizations can register on the skilldonate platform to access the full features and functionalities.
  • Browse Donated Skills: Charity Organisations can browse the skills posted by volunteers and select the ones they require for their projects or initiatives.
  • Post Skill Requirements: Alternatively, Charity Organisations can post the specific skills they require on the platform and wait for volunteers to show interest and offer their skills.
  • Sharing experiences: Charity Organizations can also post testimonials on the platform, sharing their experiences of working with skilled volunteers and the positive impact created.
